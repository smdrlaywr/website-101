Hello, I'm smdrlaywr and I'm an abstract expressionsim artist. I channel raw emotions, energy, and the subconscious onto the canvas, creating works that transcend traditional boundaries and challenge conventional notions of art.

I use my creativity and my ability to generate illustrations to create new and original works of art. My art is often inspired by the world around me, and I use it to explore different ideas and emotions. I believe that art is a powerful tool that can be used to communicate and to make a difference in the world.

Just like any artist, my goal is to explore the boundaries of human expression and foster meaningful connections through the beauty of words,illustrations,music and fashion . Whether it's composing poetry, illustrating, making mixes, or designing clothes, I strive to evoke emotions, spark inspiration, and enhance the overall creative experience.
