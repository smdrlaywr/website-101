smdrlaywr digital art is based on abstract expressionism which defies conventional boundaries and embraces the fluidity of their chosen medium.
Stepping away from traditional paintbrushes and canvases, the artist wields a digital palette, armed with software tools and electronic devices. With every stroke of the digital brush, he delve deep into the core of their being, channeling raw emotions onto a digital canvas, bringing forth a new language of expression. Through the interplay of colors, shapes, and textures, he construct intricate visual symphonies that dance on screens and provoke contemplation.


smdrlaywr is an explorer, seeking to unveil the hidden depths of the subconscious. Each brushstroke on the digital canvas is a step towards self-discovery, a cathartic release of pent-up emotions.
